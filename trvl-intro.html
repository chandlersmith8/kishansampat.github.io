Intro to my travel blog

Published: Thu 21 May 2020
By Kishan Sampat

In Travel Blog.

tags: travel blog intro

I love traveling! (who doesn’t?). This blog is a brief collection of memories from my travels. You’ll mostly find “how-to-do” / “what-to-do” information in my blog. I love giving directions and telling people about how they could spend their time at a destination and how they could could do it, like navigating through public transportation or finding off the path restaurants, or simply finding those beautiful spots where one could just sit and enjoy the beauty of the place they are in!

I hope you find this information helpful and get inspired to travel to these beautiful destinations!
